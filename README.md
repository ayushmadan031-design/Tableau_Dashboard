# 📺 Amazon Prime Video Dashboard | Tableau

## 📌 Project Overview

This Tableau dashboard provides an interactive analysis of Amazon Prime Video's content library. It helps users explore movies and TV shows based on ratings, genres, release years, countries, and content distribution.

The dashboard offers key insights into the platform's content catalog and viewing classifications through visually appealing charts and maps.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze the distribution of Movies and TV Shows.
- Identify the most common content ratings.
- Explore popular genres available on Prime Video.
- Examine content growth over time.
- Visualize content availability across different countries.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards
- **Total Titles:** 9,655
- **Total Ratings:** 25
- **Total Genres:** 519
- **Total Directors:** 5,771
- **Latest Content Year:** 2021

### Visualizations

#### 1. Ratings by Total Shows
Displays the number of shows available under different content ratings such as:
- 13+
- 16+
- 18+
- PG-13
- R
- ALL

#### 2. Genres by Total Shows
Shows the most popular genres on Prime Video including:
- Drama
- Comedy
- Documentary
- Animation & Kids
- Suspense

#### 3. Content Distribution by Country
Interactive world map displaying the number of titles available across countries.

#### 4. Movies vs TV Shows
Donut chart showing content type distribution:
- Movies: 7.81K (80.82%)
- TV Shows: 1.85K (19.18%)

#### 5. Total Shows by Release Year
Line chart illustrating the growth of Prime Video content over time.

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Movies dominate the platform, accounting for over **80%** of all content.
- **13+** is the most common content rating.
- **Drama** is the leading genre on Prime Video.
- Content production increased significantly after 2010.
- The United States contributes the largest share of content.
